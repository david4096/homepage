    Privacy notice

    This website DOES NOT use cookies to track your activity. Since this Web site is hosted on the ipfs, it is subject to a variety of new attack vectors that may threaten your privacy. I'm not responsible if something bad happens with your personal data. For example, since we only publish the keys for ipfs contents at first, it should be possible to identify the downloaders who access hashes that haven't been distributed throughout the network because our daemon is the only host. Furthermore, if you visited this site using the `ipfs.io` service, you should refer to the ipfs.io legal policy on that. Web pages should be about getting you the information relevant to you, not collecting data on you, and this one will always strive for that.
